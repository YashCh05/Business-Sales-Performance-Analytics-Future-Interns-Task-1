## ğŸ” Key Insights & Business Outcomes

- The business shows **consistent growth**, especially after 2015.
- **Technology** is the most profitable category, while **Furniture** has weak margins despite high sales.
- Certain sub-categories (Tables, Bookcases, Supplies) are **loss-making** and require attention.
